INGREDIENTS


2 red apple
2 green apple
2 oranges
2 pears
2 peaches
2 plum
1 cup pomegranate pearls
1 lemon
2 cups orange juice
1 liter water or soft drink of your choice
ice cubes as required

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. n a mixing bowl add the chopped fruits, drizzle lemon juice, and set aside.
  2. Into the large pitcher or punch bowl add the fruits, orange juice, pomegranate juice, and soft drink or water.
  3. Stir well to combine. Add ice cubes and serve chilled.

Fruit Punch

6 servings       10 minutes

Fruit punch is an assortment of drinks, either non-alcoholic or alcoholic along with fruits or fruit juices. Normally it is made in big punch bowls and served in very large quantities at parties and get-togethers. I have had fruit punch for the first time at a friend’s house party and I just loved it. I have been making it ever since during summer times when the weather is really warm and we want to slurp in something chilled.
